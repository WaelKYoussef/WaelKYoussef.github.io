Cairo Festival City Sales Center

UWP touch table & desktop application

Roles:
Start to finish Windows (UWP) Developer

Technologies:
Windows, UWP, C# & Bing Maps

Description:

The app provides a user-friendly platform for the sales team to explore and present all of the units in the projects) to clients.
The app will also help the team showcase the unit locations and details directly onto the interactive masterplan, while also helping the client visualize the actual units by watching videos, images, and the 360 virtual tours.
The app is compatible with Android tablets & Windows
10, 4K touch screens.

Every inch of the user interface is carefully crafted for intuition and ease of use.
There are multiple ways to navigate back and forth between the Districts, Types and Units within the project(s). Whether it the main side menu or the bottom bread-crumbs; User Experience has always been one of the strongest advantages of our design.
The interactive masterplan automatically moves, animates and highlight appropriately with the user's input.

Personal notes:

The first project I've developed for UWP (Universal Windows Platform). This project was the ultimate proof that knowledge and experience carry over quite well from one front-end platform to another.

Additionally, it was a grand opportunity to work on a huge 65" touch display as apposed to regular small mobile screen sizes.

It was wonderful experiencing how freeing it is developing an application for a desktop computer and a powerful OS like windows.

Newgiza | Desktop

Cross-platform desktop application

Roles:
Designer, Team leader & Lead Java Developer

Technologies:
Java, JavaFX & GoogleMaps & ArcGIS

Description:

Welcome to the Official NEWGIZA desktop application. Using the app, you can:
· Explore the entire project through our GIS powered dynamic map.
· View within the map neighbourhoods, facilities, all roads leading to NEWGIZA, project entrances, and nearby landmarks.
· Tap on any building or area to find out what it is.
· Intelligent, project wide search of all areas, buildings and landmarks.
· View residential neighbourhoods, all their unit types, renders, floor plans, and locate each area on the dynamic map.
· View all facilities, their location, ways to engage with the operator including NEWGIZA Sports Club, NEWGIZA University, and more.
· Browse virtual tours, videos, photographs and view latest construction updates.

Personal notes:

This was an exciting venture as it was my first experience developing a cross-platform desktop application. It allowed me first-hand to experience the pros and cons of cross-platform.

Additionally, it was a wonderful example in re-imagining a design meant for mobile int a desktop app without losing the integrity of the design.

It's also worth noting that my experience in developing UI 100% through code served greatly in following the same patterns and practices in developing this app with JavaFX also 100% through code.

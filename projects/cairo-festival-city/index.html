Roles:
Start to finish iOS Developer

Technologies:

Description:

THE PREMIER MIXED USE URBAN COMMUNITY IN NEW CAIRO
Spanning over a land area of 3 million square meters, Cairo Festival City is a visionary mixed use urban community strategically located at the gateway to New Cairo city. Cairo Festival City sets new standards as Egypt’s premier indoor-outdoor shopping, dining and entertainment destination, providing spectacular residential villas and luxurious apartments, as well as prime office spaces, together with internationally renowned hotels, education and automobile showrooms all set within a beautifully landscaped and tranquil environment.

A truly distinctive lifestyle experience where meticulous attention to detail is obvious and all elements of the project are seamlessly interconnected.
Strategic location

Cairo Festival City is strategically located on the East side of greater Cairo Ring Road near the districts of Maadi to the South and Heliopolis, Nasr City as well as Mokattam to the West and less than 30 minutes drive from downtown Cairo, making it easily accessible to all suburbs.

Personal notes:

Near and dear to my heart. This original Cairo Festival City application was my gateway to much more complex projects and skills.

This application featured completely custom UI, 3D effects -thanks to my experience with OpenGL-, fantastic interactive mall maps, an offline 'C' implementation of in-mall navigation that was cross compatible with Android's native SDK ... the list goes on.

This application evolved so much over time: UI, UX and Architecture. And would remain the vessel for so much development and technologies throughout its lifetime.

Timelapse Capture System

Raspberry Pi

Roles:
Designer & Sole Developer

Technologies:
Raspberry Pi, Linux, Python, Google Drive, MySQL & PHP

Description:

Introducing our innovative Timelapse Capture System, designed to help you easily capture stunning timelapse footage! The system is powered by the Raspberry Pi, which acts as the controller, and an action camera that captures high-quality images. Our system is easy to use, simply set the desired time interval and duration of the timelapse, and let the system do the rest. The Raspberry Pi will automatically trigger the action camera to capture images at the specified intervals, saving them onto a local storage device.

In addition, the system is designed to automatically send the captured images to a remote server for storage and backup. This ensures that your timelapse footage is safe and secure, even if something happens to your local storage device.

Our Timelapse Capture System is perfect for capturing the beauty of nature, construction projects, or any other long-term project. With the Raspberry Pi's low power consumption and the action camera's durability, this system can run for extended periods of time without interruption. Whether you're a professional photographer or a hobbyist, our Timelapse Capture System is an easy-to-use and reliable tool for capturing stunning timelapse footage.

Description:

Welcome to the Official mobile application of Better Home where we strive to become Egypt’s most valuable and innovative property developer that delivers world-class products and services and creates enormous value for its homeowners, shareholders and the economy.
Using the app allows you to fully explore all our residential and commercial projects, search units, make payments, view unit types, browse images, and watch videos. The GIS powered masterplan is fully interactive with real time GPS, and driving directions to any of our projects. Tap on any of the buildings for detailed information on the map, along with powerful offline search for any project or unit.
Easily and safely create and access your account only by having your mobile phone.
Find your dream unit: Easily search for and find your dream residential, commercial or administrative unit. View its details, price, floor-plans or even reserve it right through the app.
Profile: sign up for a profile to be able to view all your units, app payment history and make installment or deposit payments for any unit.

Personal notes:

Betterhome is a project that only got better and better with time, always moving forward with additional enhancements and features.

This application was originally written in Objective-C and later completely re-written with Swift & UIKit. It's also moved to use the new backend core developed entirely in pure PHP, custom made to serve as a bedrock for applications developed on multiple frontends.

This application was also a great experience in working with user-accounts, end-to-end encryption and E-Payments. Both on the frontend and backend.

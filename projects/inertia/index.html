Description:

Inertia is a top real estate company in Egypt. We make unique and valuable projects all over the country. We started in 2007 and have been successful ever since. Our projects are designed for modern people, whether they're in Cairo, by the Mediterranean Sea, or on the Red Sea. We want to build communities, not just properties. As part of the Inertia Holding Group, we only pick the best projects that offer great homes and a modern lifestyle.

Our app helps you explore all of our places to live and work. You can search for places to stay, keep up to date with construction, see what they look like, and watch videos about them. The map on the app shows you all of our projects and gives you driving directions using GPS. You can also click on any building to get more information. The app works without an internet connection so you can use it anywhere.

Personal notes:

INERTIA was a data intensive application as it targeted seven different developments. Each with their own media and GIS dataset.

Additionally, its design required a lot of interesting animations and techniques to conform to INERITA's corporate identity.

It was also an interesting experience working closely with a local IT team to setup the app's backend server.

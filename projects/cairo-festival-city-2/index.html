iOS mobile application

Roles:

Technologies:

Description:

Cairo Festival City features the ultimate standards in residential, retail, leisure, entertainment and dining in an eco-friendly, self-sustaining 'city within a city', bringing together unique, ground-breaking innovations. Enjoy a lifestyle where all your choices are at hand, where the imagination and expertise of developers Al-Futtaim has created Egypt's premier mixed-use development, extending over three million square meters at the gateway to New Cairo alongside the east of Cairo's Ring Road. Cairo Festival City's residents bask in the splendid setting, high class facilities and security encompassing accommodation from majestic villas to a huge range of apartments.

*This application is for residential and offices use only, it features minimal information about components in the city.

Personal notes:

The next step for Cairo Festival City; this application features completely new UI, UX and architecture. It's truly an entirely new application.

This is the first application to be written in Swift since becoming ABI stable (Version 5).

This is also the first application to be based on the new backend core developed entirely in pure PHP, custom made to serve as a bedrock for applications developed on multiple frontends.

EGIPA Survey

iOS mobile application

Roles:
Start to finish iOS Developer

Technologies:
iOS, Swift, Objective-C, C, UIKit, GCD & GoogleMaps

Description:

Introducing "EGIPA Survey", the app designed to help you survey and collect data on points of interest and landmarks. With EGIPA Survey, you can easily create custom surveys to collect information on everything from historical landmarks to popular tourist attractions. The app allows you to take photos and input text data to create a comprehensive database of information on each landmark. With EGIPA Survey, you can easily manage your data and export it to popular GIS and mapping software. Whether you're conducting research, creating travel guides, or managing tourist attractions, EGIPA Survey makes it easy to collect and manage your data on points of interest and landmarks.

Personal notes:

EGIPA Survey is a passion project turned indispensable tool for the GIS team. It made the complicated job of GIS surveys much simpler and streamlined than ever before.

It became so reliable to the extent that Mapillary-like image capture functionality was implemented into it for the survey teams to have full control over captured data.

Working on geo-captured imagery in particular was challenging as it pushed phones to their physical limits in terms of storage, memory, temperature and battery. Limits usually not taken into account for modern phones.

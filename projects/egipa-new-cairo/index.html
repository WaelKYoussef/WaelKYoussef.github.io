EGIPA New Cairo

iOS mobile application

Roles:
Start to finish iOS Developer

Technologies:
iOS, Swift, Objective-C, C, UIKit, GoogleMaps, PHP, MySql & ArcGIS

Description:

EGIPA New Cairo is the result of 3 years of super hard work to bring our beloved users the MOST detailed map of New Cairo. with over 44,000 Addressed buildings, 2200+ landmarks, a full street map of all of Cairo, and the latest available satellite imagery, EGIPA New Cairo is by far the only app you need to explore this vibrant city. Created by Digital Egypt, this bilingual (Arabic/English) application is loaded with amazing functionalities and features that will amaze you.

1. If your GPS is enabled and you’re in New Cairo, you can quickly explore all interesting spots around you
2. Find all commercial stores by selecting from predefined searches from the main menu
3. Get full contact details of all stores and easily add them to your phone’s contacts
4. Calculate the distance from your current location to any building or spot
5. Generate driving directions, and let the map guide you to your location
6. View the latest satellite imagery commercially available today
7. Explore area imageries, and 360 degree panoramas (Panoramas currently cover all of the fifth settlement area only)
8. The most advanced, bilingual search with Artificial Intelligence like capabilities. 9. Search for anything, a building, an area or a store. You can type “155 Fifth” to get all buildings in the Fifth Settlement that are numbered 155. Search for “fish” or “seafood” to get all seafood restaurants.
10. Share any content you see with your friends, the share link generated opens in the web application, so that any user can open it, with options to view the shared content on the mobile app
11. Quickly zoom the map to predefined areas in New Cairo
12. Customize the look and feel of the app by changing the color from the main menu and changing the visible map layers (Map only, Hybrid or Satellite only)
13. Tilt the map in 3D for a more immersive experience. Swipe the map up with two fingers.

Personal notes:

EGIPA New Cairo was my very first application written in Swift since version 2.0. It's also a map centric app (like Google Maps) that featured an abundance of GIS data.

EGIPA New Cairo's niche was to handle huge set of GIS data and cater them to wonderful features like navigation and the augmented reality explorer.

It was also a great experience working with 100% custom UI, a custom language manager and a custom theme manager.

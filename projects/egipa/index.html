Description:

EGIPA is the result of 6 years of super hard work to bring our beloved users the richest interactive map of Egypt. with over 5 million Addressed buildings, 200,000+ points of interest, a full street map and the latest available satellite imagery, EGIPA is by far the only app you need to explore this vibrant country. Created by Intercept Technologies, this bilingual (Arabic/English) application is loaded with amazing functionalities and features that will amaze you.

Features:

Search and Explore:
- Quickly explore the area you’re in or any other area simply by moving the map around. You can find POIs, addresses and streets with a simple swipe of a finger.
- Advanced, bilingual search allows you to find anything; an address, a building, an area or a place you’re interested in. EGIPA supports Arabic, English and even mixed searches.
- Search by quickly selecting a type or POI you’re interested in right-now. Not only will EGIPA show you all that’s available in the area, but you can move the map around to explore more of the same type of POIs.
- Power-users can make use of powerful search filters to help them pin-point the absolute matches for their searches.

Engage:
- Get full contact details of all stores and easily interact with them by leaving a review on your favorite places.
- Contribute to the community by sharing your own images of places and enhance the experience of others.
- Share any content you see with your friends, with a simple click, from any POI, address, street or even a favorite place you previously saved.

Tailored Experience:
- Select what type of POIs you are interested in and EGIPA will explore only what is interesting to you.
- Add your favorite and frequent locations as a Favourite place so you may visit them instantly anytime you want.
- Change your Language or Color theme preferences at anytime from EGIPA’s setting page.
- Search history remembers your latest searches and remembers them just in-case you want to re-visit them soon.

Easy access:
- Sign-in instantly with your Apple, Google or Facebook accounts.
- if you would prefer, sign-up for an EGIPA-only account with your e-mail in under 90 seconds.


What’s next?

EGIPA is an ever-growing product which aims at continuously enhancing the users’ experience and convenience. Not only are we always working on bettering the aforementioned feature set, we also plan on expanding EGIPA with amazing features making it one of a kind.

Personal notes:

EGIPA is one of these super projects that is so near and dear to my heart. I technically started on EGIPA around 10 years prior with EGIPA New Cairo. Not only is EGIPA a complete re-write from the original, it's to the point that there isn't a single line of code shared between the two.

One of the best experiences I've ever had through EGIPA is the backend architecture of which I had the privilege to completely design and implement myself. EGIPA utilizes multiple architectural design patterns and techniques to be able to expand to handle virtually hundreds of thousands of users concurrently.

Another one of the best challenges I'm glad to have tackled is the simple problem of Search, which turned out to be anything but simple. I loved being able to mix English, Arabic and Geographical searching and combining them in interesting ways to present a very useful product that I personally find myself intuitively using way more than any other similar apps.

EGIPA to me is the ideal software product for any developer to work on, a software product which satisfies my end-user side way more than my developer side. It's even better to know the future of EGIPA and all the wonderful plans Intercept has in-store ... it's nothing short of exciting!!!

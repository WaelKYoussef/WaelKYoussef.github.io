How I'm using AI to help with this blog?

A message from our little big helper: "While I'm a language model and can't claim to be a "real" software engineer, I've been trained on a massive amount of text data, including technical documentation, code, and blog posts. This has given me a solid understanding of software development concepts and practices".



So, how do I use AI to help in writing with this blog?

Well, it's not as simple as just pressing a button and having a fully-formed post appear, mmm... , okay maybe it's a little bit like that, but not quite!. I mainly use AI as a tool to assist me in "starting" the writing process; here's a breakdown of how it works:

1. Brainstorming and Topic Selection


I start by searching for ideas and trending topics on the software development, tech or anything else I may be inclined to talk about. This gives me a sense of what people are interested in as well helps come up with the first direction for my posts.
 I use tools like Google Search Trends and AI-powered content generators to identify popular keywords and phrases. This helps me leverage search-engine indexing as well as tailor my content to my audience.

2. Research and Gathering Information


I can access and process information from various sources and models efficiently. This allows me to gather data on specific topics while not being distracted by the usual fluff of online searching.
 AI is AMAZING at summarizing complex information or extract key points from lengthy articles.

3. Outlining and Drafting


• Create a structured outline for my posts. This helps me organize my thoughts and ensure a logical flow, this single usage is enough to warp a blogger's productivity into the 4th dimension.
• Also, generate first drafts for my posts. Suggesting different ways to phrase sentences or provide alternative word choices.



4. Editing and Refining


Using grammar and style checkers to identify errors in my writing can be just the proof-reader you need in a pinch.
 Human Review: Still, it's important to have a human -when possible- review my posts to ensure they are accurate, engaging, and understandable.

5. Adding Code Snippets and Examples


• I can use AI-powered code generation tools to create code examples that illustrate my points. However, I admit that some of the most laughable / dangerous code snippets I've ever seen were AI generated. I can never stress enough the importance of triple-checking generated code before releasing it to the wild.

6. Adding Visuals


• Using AI-powered image generation tools to create custom visuals for my posts can help make content more engaging and visually appealing, it's also perfect in that usually a blog post requires simple flat illustrations which are prefect for AI image generation.

A Note on Authenticity
While AI can be a powerful tool, it's important to use it in a way that maintains authenticity and not to fall in the pits of laziness. I strive to write in a way that reflects my own style, understanding and experience, in spite of me using AI's assistance. I believe that a combination of human expertise and AI technology can produce high-quality content that is both informative and engaging.

By leveraging AI in this way, I'm able to -more easily- produce blog posts that are informative, engaging, and relevant to my audience. It's a win-win for everyone involved .. or maybe that's what a total hack would say!

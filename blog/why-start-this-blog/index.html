Why start this blog?

A Journey of Sharing and Learning





Hello World, and hello to you, fellow developers! It's great to have you here. I'm so excited to finally launch this blog. I've been thinking about doing this for a while now, and now after about half an ion, I'm finally getting around working on it.

So, why am I starting this blog? Well, there are a few reasons. First, I love sharing my knowledge and experience with others. I've been coding for over 14 years now, and I've learned a lot along the way. I want to pass that knowledge and experience on to anyone who may benefit from it, whether you're a developer just starting out, looking to level up or just a plain weirdo.

Second, I wanted a space where I can just write whatever I want however I want it, no social media or anyone else's platform. I believe that learning and thought sharing should be fun and engaging. That's why I'll be -to the best of my ability- using clear and concise language and keep to being casual and approachable, so you don't feel like you're reading a textbook.



What Can You Expect from This Blog?



You can expect to find blog posts on a wide variety of topics, including:

• Programming languages and frameworks: I'll be covering everything from the basics of Swift & PHP to mor advanced topics like System design and platform architecture.
• Software development best practices: I'll share tips and tricks for writing clean, maintainable, sexy code.
• Career advice: I'll offer advice on everything from landing your first job to negotiating a salary and climbing up through your career.
• Personal anecdotes: I'll share stories from my own experiences as a software engineer, ones that are not covered by an NDA.



Let's Get Started!



I'm really excited to see where this blog goes. I hope you'll join me on this journey. Feel free to leave a comment below and let me know what you'd like to see more of.

Until next time, happy coding!
